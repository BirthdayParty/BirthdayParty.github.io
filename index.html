OUR BELIEFS AND CAMPAIGNS

The people before the power

YOUR Rights

To us, this includes keeping and a continuing relationship with people no matter the colour, race or cultural identity.

YOUR Health

We will change public healthcare to being free and private hospital care still available if the people desire.

YOUR Finance

We will boost funding for housing and make higher wages, regardless to gender identity.

Renewable and Cheap Electricity

We will reduce electricity costs and fund more money on renewable electricity for a brighter future for Australia.

More Infrastructure Funding

We will fund towards the development of infrastructure for Australia.

More Jobs (Infrastructure)

Our infrastructure funding means that more jobs will be created every year to make Australia the place to be.

OUR VISION FOR AUSTRALIA

THE PEOPLE BEFORE THE POWER

Our vision for Australia is to rebuild the communities whilst helping the economy to increase. We aim to rebuild families destroyed by debt and health issues surrounding jobs and infrastructure. We want to build more infrastructure making more jobs for Australians which could take the financial stress of crippled families. Peoples rights includes equal jobs and pay opportunities for both men and women. We also want to invest in more renewable electricity so Australia does not have to burn fossil fuels.

These things are going to make Australia the place to live and be, this also means that people can live in a biased free environment.
